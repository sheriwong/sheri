Style Profile is an independent and responsive SaaS product where fashion brands are able to integrate into their eCommerce website. The end user is able to define their fashion identity by uploading and choosing their favourite styles. Style Profile will learn from their inputs and show them the most relevant product recommendations.

Problem Space

Eighty percent of customers are having trouble finding styles that they like in online stores. If the stores have recommendations, they are not accurate and/or personalized.

PERSONAS

I've identified two user personas for this project, Angela Banks, an online shopper, and Olivia Woods, an omnichannel director who is a potential client for Style Profile. Angela's goals are to receive a seamless shopping experience with recommendations generated by AI algorithms that target her personal style. For Olivia, her goal is to add value to her company by driving sales and social media presence through their eCommerce website.

User Interviews

The user insights were gathered from 6 Millenial online shoppers and 3 marketing managers.

Research insights from online shoppers:

  • 100% of users have certain themes/styles of clothing in their closets
  • 100% of users have found clothing that they like but their size was unavailable on multiple occasions
  • 100% of users browse on a clothing website if there is a sale or discount code
  • 100% of users are concerned about fit when shopping online
  • 80% of users go to a shopping website to browse, without a specific item in mind
  • 80% of users experience frustration with looking for styles that they like
  • 80% of users don’t mind doing 1-2 minute quizzes to receive personal recommendations
  • 80% of users are influenced by upselling and cross-selling widgets
  • 40% of users bought an item through an ad based on the style they like
  • 40% of users find it difficult to find styles they like because they don’t know the name of the style

Research insights from potential clients:

  • The application must integrate with their website’s style and brand guidelines
  • Sizing of product images, fonts, font colour should be customizable
  • Customizable placement of application
  • Demo of Style Profile must reflect a strong brand presence
  • Accurate recommendations are important
  • Social media integration is a 'nice to have'

Final Features

Style Profile Integration + Style quiz

The Style Profile is marketed on the homepage of Aritzia's homepage. The user is also able to access Style Profile under their profile. Once the user lands on Style Profile, they are prompted to complete a style quiz which is step one in receiving personalized recommendations.

Upload your style

The user is able to upload their fashion inspiration in two ways, through their/Aritzia’s Instagram or from their device. The most recent uploads show up first. The user is able to expand, collapse, and remove their uploads. After receiving recommendations, the user is able to style the item with other clothing matches. If the user wants more personalized styling advice, they are able to send their Style Profile to a style advisor. This function connects the company’s stylists with customers.

Choose your style

The user is able to like and dislike products to refine their recommendations. If the user really likes the product item, they’re able to add the item into their wishlist or right-click to go to the item’s product page. Same with all the recommendations, the user is able to explore styling matches with the items they like.

Learning Outcomes

I learned that I shouldn’t design a solution by just relying on one type of stakeholder. In my problem space, I had to consider the shoppers and the client’s goals. In the initial wireframes, all the tasks were on the same tab. After feedback, I reworked the information architecture and divided each task into a separate tab to avoid information overload and improved the user flow. The next step would be to design the dashboard platform for the client. This includes the application’s settings, billing, API instructions, analytics and many more.
